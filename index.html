For Always

For once, you opened your soul
And I saw a universe of gentle storms and silent dreams.

For once, you whispered the kind of love
That's not loud, but lasting

If love ever knocked again at your door,
Let it be me - not just for once,
But for always.

For always, I want to be the arms you run to
when the world turns cold and loud.

For always, I want to hold your hand
like a promise - steady, certain and unshaken.

Your fears, your tears, your silences
I don't just accept them, I treasure them.

Let it be the calm to your chaos,
The prayer you never knew was being answered.

For always, I'll remember your soul
Even in my quietest moments.

Because someone like you isn't meant to be loved "For Once"
You're meant to be loved forever.

~ Md Mazharul Haque Adib
